Evaluation

Strengths & Weaknesses

✓ Strengths

  • Best AI/ML platform — Vertex AI + Gemini + TPUs + TensorFlow
  • BigQuery is unmatched for large-scale analytics
  • Google's private global network gives superior networking speed
  • GKE is the most feature-rich managed Kubernetes
  • Persistent discount for sustained usage — no upfront commitment needed
  • Generally the most affordable compute and egress pricing
  • Committed use discounts up to 57%

✕ Weaknesses

  • Smaller service catalog than AWS or Azure
  • Google has a history of deprecating products (see: Stadia, GCP IoT Core)
  • Smaller enterprise sales and support network
  • Less Windows-native support than Azure
  • Fewer compliance certifications in some regions
  • Fewer third-party integrations in marketplace
