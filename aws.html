Evaluation

Strengths & Weaknesses

✓ Strengths

  • Widest range of services (200+) — any workload covered
  • Largest global infrastructure (34 regions)
  • Biggest community, talent pool, and third-party ecosystem
  • Market leader since 2006 — battle-tested
  • Best-in-class serverless (Lambda, Fargate)
  • Strong compliance portfolio (HIPAA, FedRAMP, SOC 2)
  • Generous free tier for experimenting

✕ Weaknesses

  • Pricing is complex and can be expensive at scale
  • Steep learning curve for beginners
  • Console UI can feel overwhelming
  • Support costs add up quickly
  • Vendor lock-in risk with proprietary services
  • Data egress charges are high
